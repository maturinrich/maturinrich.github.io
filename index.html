What is a VPN?

A VPN (Virtual Private Network) is a tool that encrypts your internet connection to ensure your privacy and security while accessing the web.

Features of a VPN

Privacy

Maintain your online anonymity and secure your sensitive data.

Security

Protect your information on public Wi-Fi networks.

Global Access

Enjoy unrestricted access to content worldwide.

How Does a VPN Work?

A VPN creates a secure, encrypted tunnel between your device and the internet, protecting your online activity from threats.
